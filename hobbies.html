Applying Data Science to My Interests

Combining my love for sports and data science allows me to track progress, analyze strategies, and explore performance metrics. By applying data science to these areas, I gain new insights into both my hobbies and potential future applications in the sports and fitness industry.

In soccer, I am particularly interested in tracking performance data to assess skill improvement over time. For e-sports, I focus on analyzing in-game strategies to identify patterns that enhance gameplay. Finally, in fitness, I use data visualization to monitor my workout progress and set achievable goals based on previous performance.

  • Soccer: Tracking performance and statistics.
  • E-sports: Analyzing in-game strategies and metrics.
  • Workout: Visualizing personal progress over time.
