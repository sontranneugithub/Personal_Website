About Me

Hello, and welcome to my personal website! My name is Son Tran, and I'm a data science enthusiast with a strong interest in data visualization and applying analytical insights to real-world scenarios. As data becomes an increasingly essential part of understanding patterns and predicting trends, I find myself captivated by the potential it holds to solve complex problems and uncover hidden insights.

Data visualization, in particular, is one of my favorite aspects of data science. It transforms raw numbers into a visual story, making complex information accessible and meaningful. I’m fascinated by how a well-designed visualization can reveal trends that might otherwise go unnoticed. In my studies and projects, I aim to hone my skills in data visualization to explore various topics, especially in fields I'm passionate about.

Outside of academics, I’m deeply interested in sports, e-sports, and fitness. Whether it’s tracking performance in soccer, analyzing strategies in competitive gaming, or monitoring workout progress, I see endless possibilities to apply data science to my hobbies. I hope this website serves as a platform to showcase my journey, projects, and how I’m integrating data science with my personal interests. Thanks for visiting, and I look forward to sharing my work with you!
